💎 How To Claim Reviewing

Complete guide to participating in drop map reviews and earning rewards

📌 Step 1: Drop Map Tester Posts → Create a Thread

A drop map tester will post in the testing-done channel. You need to create a thread on their message by clicking the "Create thread button".

Drop Map Tester
in #testing-done
New Fortnite map ready for testing!
https://fortnite.gg/?d=26/5/14/LNtpKOtJ

💬 Step 2: Write "doing" → Bot Responds

In the thread you just created, type "doing" and send it. The Wave Management Bot will immediately respond with your Response Time Bracket and Points Per Marker.

You
6:13 am
doing
🤖
Wave Management Bot
12:57 pm
⏱️ Response Time Bracket
After 1 hour
Points Per Marker: 1 pts
✅ Response tracked and points will be awarded

⚠️ Keep this in mind! The points per marker varies based on how fast you respond.

🔍 Step 3: Review the Map → Make a Thread with Results

Go to the drop map tester link and review the map. Once done, go back and create a thread on that map link. Post your review results there.

Reviewer Name
6:13 am
https://fortnite.gg/?d=26/5/14/LNtpKOtJ https://drive.proton.me/urls/JIC82YA4XC#m77QLEpa3y2Q
Fortnite Interactive Map
Reviewer Name
in thread • 6:15 am
1 good
2 marker out of bounds
3 good
4 collision issue near spawn
Overall: Good map, needs marker 4 fix

📝 Step 4: Format Your Results

Write your review results in this format — number each marker and describe what you found:

1 good

2 write result here etc

3 etc

✓ Number each marker (1, 2, 3, etc.)
✓ Describe what you found for each marker
✓ Include any notes or fixes needed

⚡ Pro Tip

Your reviewing accuracy is what matters most! Focus on providing accurate, detailed reviews to climb the ranks, earn higher multipliers, and get bigger rewards.
